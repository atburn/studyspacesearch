Welcome!

Study Space Search is a powerful application for finding study spaces designed by students, for students. A variety of useful and versatile database-driven tools are provided for finding just the right study space for any specific set of needs.

In addition to Study Space Search's capable filtering and search features, real-time comments with specific ratings for important room characteristics, as well as a feature for saving spaces, allow the community to curate the information available to Study Space Search's users dynamically.

So, need a quiet, reservable space equipped with a whiteboard for a team meeting? How about a room with plenty of space on campus that also has a microwave so you can warm up your lunch?

Study Space Search has you covered.
